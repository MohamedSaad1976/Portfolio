San Francisco city employee salary from 2011 to 2014

The San Francisco City Employee Salary Analysis project analyzes employee salaries in San Francisco from 2011 to 2014. This project provides a detailed exploration of salary data involving data extraction, visualization, and insights regarding trends in employee compensation over the specified years. The project aims to showcase the ability to analyze and report on large datasets to derive meaningful insights.
