TED Youtube Channel

The YouTube channel Analysis project involves analyzing the content and performance of the TED YouTube channel. It includes evaluating metrics such as video views, likes, comments, and overall engagement. The analysis involves web scraping or data extraction techniques to gather insights into TED's content strategy on YouTube, focusing on trends and viewer interaction. This project demonstrates data analysis application to social media platforms for deriving actionable insights related to digital content performance.
